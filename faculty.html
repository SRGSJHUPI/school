Meet Our Faculty & Staff

Dedicated mentors and guides who shape the future of our Ambedkarians

Mr. Rohit Gautam

Founder & Director

B.Tech (IIT KANPUR)

Experience: 3+ years

“Education is that sword which doesn't require shield to fight”

– Rohit Gautam

Rohit Gautam, an IIT Kanpur alumnus, is the visionary founder of Rising Nalanda. With a B.Tech degree, he is passionate about leveraging education to empower underprivileged children. His dedication to social equality and educational excellence drives the mission of Rising Nalanda, where he aims to provide quality education and opportunities to those in need, especially marginalized communities. He also believes in women empowerment and gives special attention to girl children in his school.
