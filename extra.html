Ambedkarite Values:

  • Education First: We believe education is the path to liberation. Our faculty prioritize learning, reason, and knowledge above all rituals or dogmas.
  • Self-Respect and Dignity: No student should feel inferior. We cultivate confidence, reject discrimination, and build dignity through dialogue and discipline.
  • Equality Without Compromise: Whether the barrier is caste, religion, gender, or class—our educators teach students to question, reject, and rise above all forms of inequality.
  • Critical Spirituality: Critical Spirituality We do not promote blind faith. Our faculty encourage respectful dialogue with all faiths, while empowering students to challenge injustice in any tradition.
  • Reject Slavery of Any Kind: Emotional, religious, or social manipulation has no place in our school. If anything demands your rights in exchange for loyalty, leave it behind. Liberty is non-negotiable.
  • Fraternity and Social Responsibility: Inspired by Buddha’s Sangha and Ambedkar’s movement, we promote collective progress, team learning, and mutual care among students.
  • Voice Against Injustice: Silence in the face of wrong is betrayal. Our faculty actively teach students to speak, write, and act against injustice—peacefully but firmly.
  • Scientific Temper and Rational Thinking: Decisions must be guided by reason, evidence, and logic—not by superstition or authority. Our teachers foster scientific inquiry in every subject.
  • Lifelong Learning and Evolution: No one is “fully educated.” Our faculty constantly learn, unlearn, and update their own thinking to stay accountable to students and society.
