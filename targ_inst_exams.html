Scholarship Opportunities (External)

Category A: School Level Schemes (Class 1 to 12)

1. Pre-Matric Scholarship (Minorities)

**Applicable Classes:** Class 1 to 10 (Income ceiling applies)
**Purpose:** Financial aid for students from six notified minority communities to encourage school education.
Apply via National Scholarship Portal (NSP)

2. PM-YASASVI Pre-Matric Scholarship

**Applicable Classes:** Class 9 and 10
**Purpose:** Scholarship for students belonging to OBC, EBC (Economically Backward Class), and DNT communities. (Income ceiling ₹2.5 Lakh p.a.)
Apply via National Scholarship Portal (NSP)

3. National Means Cum Merit Scholarship (NMMS)

**Applicable Classes:** Class 9 to 12
**Purpose:** Merit-cum-means scholarship for economically weaker section students to prevent dropouts after Class 8. (Awarded ₹12,000 p.a.)
Apply via National Scholarship Portal (NSP)

4. Pre-Matric Scholarship (SC, ST, OBC)

**Applicable Classes:** Varies by Caste/State (typically Class 1 to 10)
**Purpose:** Various state-implemented schemes offering financial aid to SC, ST, and OBC students. Check NSP for specific state guidelines.
Apply via National Scholarship Portal (NSP)

5. CBSE Merit Scholarship for Single Girl Child

**Applicable Classes:** Class 11 and 12
**Purpose:** Encourages the education of a single girl child in a family. Paid monthly for two years.
CBSE Scholarship Page

6. Begum Hazrat Mahal National Scholarship

**Applicable Classes:** Class 9 to 12 (Minority Girls only)
**Purpose:** Merit-cum-means scholarship exclusively for meritorious girl students belonging to minority communities.
Maulana Azad Education Foundation (MAEF)

7. INSPIRE Scheme (Science Talent)

**Applicable Classes:** Class 6 to Class 10 (For science projects/talent identification)
**Purpose:** Offers awards for students demonstrating exceptional creative thinking in science projects, encouraging research careers.
Ministry of Science & Technology

8. National Talent Search Examination (NTSE)

**Applicable Class:** Class 10 (Exam taken in 10th; provides continuous benefits)
**Purpose:** Prestigious scholarship examination to identify and nurture talented students. Provides financial support until PhD level.
NCERT Website

Category B: Higher Education Schemes (After Class 12)

1. Post-Matric Scholarships (SC/ST/OBC)

**Applicable Classes:** Class 11 and Above (Primarily for College/University level)
**Purpose:** Comprehensive financial support for SC/ST/OBC students pursuing higher education (college/degree courses).
Apply via National Scholarship Portal (NSP)

2. Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship (CSSS)

**Applicable Classes:** After Class 12 (Meritorious students from poor families)
**Purpose:** Merit-cum-means scholarship awarded based on Class 12 results for pursuing graduate/postgraduate degree courses.
Apply via National Scholarship Portal (NSP)

3. Vidyadhan Scholarship Program (Private)

**Applicable Classes:** Class 10 passed (Funds Intermediate/Degree level)
**Purpose:** Supports bright students from economically backward families for their higher education. State-specific eligibility applies.
Vidyadhan Official Website

**Important Note:** Application windows for external scholarships (like those on the NSP) typically open between **August and November** annually. Students and parents are strongly advised to check the official portals for the exact dates and specific eligibility criteria for their state and community.
