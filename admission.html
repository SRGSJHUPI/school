Admission Structure

  • Admissions will start from 1st class onwards. Admission will be given on the basis of Admission Test, financial condition of parents and other necessary parameters.

Fees and Scholarships

  • AEC(Ambedkar Excellence Certificate) based Fee exemption: On the basis of AEC certificate, if any new student take admission and uses already passed out student's AEC certificate then, a tuition fee waiver of 10% will be provided.

  • We have Two Types of Fee Payment Systems:

    Forward Fee Payment System(FFPS):

  • Standard tuition fee: â‚¹10,000 per month or 10% of income which is higher (Base Fee), (subject to revision based on operational costs). financially weaker students may get total upto 90% fee waiver on base fee.
  • An additional 10% discount on the admission fee will be granted to those who identify as Ambedkarite, as confirmed in the admission form.
  • Fee may vary depending on the class from which admission was taken (for the same class two students may have different fees depending in which class admission was taken). taking admission in earlier classes like 1st, 2nd, 3rd may help in fee reduction.

    • Reverse Fee Payment System(RFPS):

  • applicable only for financial weaker parents.
  • Basic fee (approx. Rs. 200-500) from students will be charged but if student take leave of one day without school's approval a charge of Rs. 200 will be imposed.

Parental Role and Guidelines

  • Parents must attend bi-monthly meetings and review student growth metrics.
  • Failure to attend 2 meetings may result in student deactivation for that cycle.
  • School emphasizes full cooperation from home to implement vision of education.

Complaint & Review System

The school operates a transparent grievance redressal system. Feedback can be submitted online or in writing.

  • Student ID is required for complaint submission.
  • Cases are reviewed weekly by a committee of staff and community members.
  • Students and parents may appeal decisions through open sessions.
