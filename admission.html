Admissions

Join a movement, not just a school. Education for all, justice for all.

A step towards Rationality.

Admission Structure

  • Admissions will start from 1st class onwards. Admission will be given on the basis of Admission Test, financial condition of parents and other necessary parameters.
  • Discounts on books, uniforms, bags, and other essentials will be offered to financially weaker families.
  • Parents must agree to academic flexibility and terms. Transport safety not school’s responsibility.

📘 Fees and Scholarships

      Two Types of Fee Payment System

      1. 📖 Forward Fee Payment System (FFPS)

    • Base Tuition Fee: ₹10,000 per month or 10% of income (whichever is higher). This is the Base Fee, subject to revision.
    • All exemptions or discounts are applied after calculating the Base Fee.
    • Financially stable parents are not eligible for exemptions (exceptions may apply in rare cases).

      • 🎓 Fee Exemptions & Concessions (Middle Class only)

    • 90% fee exemption for students from middle-class families with no stable income or assets.

      • 👧 Girl Child Fee Rules (irrespective of category)

    • Single Girl Child: Additional 10% waiver on Base Fee.
    • Two Girl Children: Additional 15% waiver each on Base Fee.
    • More than Two Girl Children: Additional 20% waiver each on Base Fee.

      • 2. 🔄 Reverse Fee Payment System (RFPS)

    • Basic fee of ₹200–₹500 will be charged.
    • If a student takes leave of one day without prior permission, a fine of ₹200 will be imposed.
    • Note: This is designed to ensure attendance and encourage parents in rural areas to send children regularly.

      • 🏅 Scholarships & Waivers

      📜 AEC (Ambedkar Excellence Certificate) Based Fee Exemption

    • If a new student takes admission using an AEC certificate from a passed-out student:
      ⭐ 5-star AEC → 10% tuition fee waiver
      ⭐ 4-star AEC → 8% tuition fee waiver
      ⭐ Lower ratings → proportionally lower waivers (subject to revision).
    • Students with outstanding academic achievement will be honored with a gold statue of Dr. B.R. Ambedkar.

      • 📌 Early Admission Fee Exemption

    • Students admitted in earlier classes (e.g., Class 1, 2, 3) will receive an additional 5% waiver on the Base Fee.

      • 🙌 Work-Based Fee Exemption

    • Families unable to afford the fees may contribute through volunteer work (school maintenance, administrative assistance, etc.)

      • Fee Payment Rules

      💰 Flexible Payment Plans

    • Quarterly or annual fee payment options are available with 1% interest, applicable only for financially weaker families (exceptions excluded).

      • 🏠 Property & Asset Clause

    • Families with sufficient property and assets must pay the full fee, even without a stable income.

      • ⏰ Late Fee Penalty

    • A penalty will be imposed for late payment of fees.

Parental Role and Guidelines

  • Parents must attend bi-monthly meetings and review student growth metrics.
  • Failure to attend 2 meetings may result in student deactivation for that cycle.
  • School emphasizes full cooperation from home to implement vision of education.

Complaint & Review System

The school operates a transparent grievance redressal system. Feedback can be submitted online or in writing.

  • Student ID is required for complaint submission.
  • Cases are reviewed weekly by a committee of staff and community members.
  • Students and parents may appeal decisions through open sessions.
