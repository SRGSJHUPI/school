Vision & Mission
Our Vision

To empower Ambedkarites—making them sharp-minded, compassionate, socially responsible, and business-minded individuals who excel in academics, sports, and research.
Our vision: Empower students, particularly from reserved categories, to achieve academic excellence on par with the general category in national-level exams and secure positions in top institutions like IITs, AIIMS, and NLU, while also preparing them for global opportunities and entrepreneurial success.

Our Mission

Establish a school in Greater Noida that provides high-quality education to underprivileged children, focusing on academic excellence and holistic development.
Inspired by Dr. B.R. Ambedkar: We aim to bridge educational disparities by offering resources, guidance, and support to students—especially from SC, ST, and OBC (Ambedkarites only) communities. We promote educational equity, instill values of compassion and justice, and prepare students to become empowered and responsible citizens.

How We Are Different from Other Schools
Not for Profit Model:
  • We focus on educating society, not making profit.
  • Discounts on essentials for financially weaker families.
  • We avoid unnecessary yearly book changes to reduce burden.
Advanced & Personalized Academics:
  • Academics designed by an IITian—leadership beyond the board.
  • Focus on brain development and joy of learning.
  • Skill-building in sports and research classes (robotics, aircraft, etc.).
National & International Exam Preparation:
  • Early exposure to JEE, NEET, Olympiads, NTSE, KVPY, and more.
  • Preparation for TOEFL, GRE, and international admissions.
Technology & Innovation:
  • Comprehensive tech learning, research projects, web/app development.
  • Machine learning, data structures, algorithms, competitive programming.
Inclusive Fee Structure:
  • 10% income-based fee for affordability (T&C apply).
