Terms & Conditions

1. Admission Policy

Admission is subject to seat availability and successful completion of the registration process. Submission of a registration form does not guarantee admission.

2. Seat Allocation

Seats are allotted on a first-come, first-served basis. Students may be placed on a waiting list if seats are unavailable.

3. Batch Formation

Rising Nalanda reserves the right to postpone, merge, split, reschedule, or cancel any batch if the required minimum number of students is not achieved or due to operational reasons.

4. Fee Policy

Fees must be paid within the prescribed time. Failure to pay fees may result in suspension or cancellation of admission.

5. Refund Policy

Fees once paid are generally non-refundable except in exceptional circumstances approved by the management.

6. Attendance

Students are expected to attend classes regularly. Repeated absenteeism may affect academic progress and participation in classroom activities.

7. Student Conduct

Students must maintain discipline and treat teachers, staff, and fellow students with respect. Bullying, abusive behavior, cheating, harassment, or disruption of classes may lead to disciplinary action.

8. Academic Performance

The institution provides guidance, teaching, and academic support. However, examination results depend on individual effort, attendance, aptitude, and study habits.

9. No Guaranteed Results

Rising Nalanda does not guarantee specific marks, ranks, board results, Olympiad results, JEE, NEET, NDA, CUET, or any other examination outcome.

10. Faculty & Schedule Changes

Faculty assignments, class timings, venues, teaching methods, curriculum structure, and schedules may be modified whenever necessary without prior notice.

11. Study Material

Notes, assignments, recordings, presentations, and study material provided by the institution are for enrolled students only and may not be copied, distributed, sold, or published without permission.

12. Accuracy of Information

Students and parents must provide accurate information during registration. Admission may be cancelled if false information is discovered.

13. Personal Property

The institution is not responsible for loss, theft, or damage of personal belongings brought to classes or events.

14. Privacy

Student and parent information collected during registration shall be used only for academic, administrative, communication, and institutional purposes.

15. Website Information

Information displayed on the website is provided in good faith. The institution reserves the right to update, modify, or remove any content without prior notice.

16. Acceptance of Terms

By registering, enrolling, making payments, or using this website, students and parents agree to comply with these Terms & Conditions.
