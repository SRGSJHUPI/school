Welcome To Rising Nalanda

Empowering Justice and Excellence
Vision & Mission

🌟Vision:
To create an inclusive educational ecosystem where every child is empowered with ethical values, scientific thinking, and global readiness — enabling them to rise as confident, compassionate, and socially responsible individuals. We envision a future where education dismantles inequality and builds a society led by wisdom, courage, and dignity.

🎯Mission:
- To provide concept-driven, inclusive advanced education rooted in scientific temper, ethics, and social awareness.
- To prepare students for top national-level exams like JEE, NEET, CLAT, and admission into premier institutions such as IITs, AIIMS, and NLUs.
- To integrate timeless values — like justice, equality, rationality, and compassion — into daily learning and decision-making.
- To ensure that no child is left behind due to financial weaker background — by creating a supportive, empowering academic environment.
- To cultivate leaders, researchers, and change-makers who carry forward a legacy of social responsibility, humanistic values, and courageous thinking.

How we are different from other schools
Not for profit Model:
  • we are focusing to educate society instead making profit.
  • Discounts on books, uniforms, bags, and other essentials will be offered to financially weaker families.
  • To reduce financial burden of parents we try not to change books yearly.
Advanced and personalized academics:
  • Rising Beyond the Board toward leadership since School is owned by an IITian and Academics is designed by him. Make students to stand in Competitive Environment
  • We focus on brain development along with exams preparation, encouraging students not just to aim for success in exams but to embrace the joy of learning and make the process enjoyable.
  • ensure each student becomes skilled in at least one game (e.g., badminton, chess, basketball) (will be implement later if sufficient fund…)
  • Special Research classes will involve hands-on projects—such as building robots, designing aircraft, and solving complex problems—that require research and critical thinking.
National & International Exam Preparation at school level:
  • Early Exposure to JEE, NEET, Olympiad, NTSE, KVPY & other national and International Platforms
  • prepare students for international exams like TOEFL and GRE too, which helps in getting abroad admission which lacks even in high profile schools as far as I have looked into it.
Inclusive Fee Structure:
  • A 10% income-based fee ensures affordability for all families, encouraging access to quality education irrespective of socio-economic status (will be implemented later T&C apply).
  • We have introduced Forward Fee System(FFS) and Reverse Fee System(RFS) Click here for more Information
Building a Strong Foundation in Technology & Innovation:
  • Comprehensive technology learning & research projects
  • introduction to web development and app development
  • Machine learning
  • Data Structure and algorithm
  • competitive programming
Some Other Special Quality
Future guidance:
  • Guidance by IITian for Engineering students and by AIIMS doctor for medical students will be provided.
We offer open courses:
  • We will offer courses like robotics, machine learning, computer vision, web development, app development, languages (e.g. Japanese) at basic level. Other school’s students can also join these courses.
Holistic Education & Social Values:
  • In addition to academics, students are encouraged to excel in sports, research, and special focus on societal values, shaping them into well-rounded individuals.
Parental Involvement & Feedback:
  • Complaint/Suggestion portal for students & parents will be there so that parents can suggest improvements. School will take final decision on that.
Unique Features
Excellence Certificate (AEC):
  • Students performing outstanding will get this certificate, this certificate not only honors academic and personal excellence but also offers a tuition fee waiver for new admissions, promoting a cycle of inspiration and equity.
Global Language Learning:
  • Students have the opportunity to learn international languages like Japanese, Spanish, and French, expanding their cultural horizons and global communication skills. (will be implemented later).
Inclusive Education Rooted in Social Justice:
  • Inspired by Dr. B.R. Ambedkar, we promote an inclusive environment where students from all backgrounds thrive with dignity and equality.
Taught by Passionate Educators & IITians:
  • With guidance from mentors who have cleared top exams and walked the same road, students get real insight and direction.
Moral Development & Discipline:
  • With a strong focus on values, time respect, and empathy, we shape students into socially responsible human beings, not just high scorers.
Founder's Motivation
  • I am deeply passionate about teaching, and I genuinely love being around children. A school is the one place where I can educate, empower, and play with young minds—making it my ideal space to give back to society.
  • When I secured admission to IIT Kanpur with an All India Rank around 21,500, I was overjoyed — but it also opened my eyes to a harsh truth. In many villages like mine, even parents and students are unaware of institutes like IITs, AIIMS, or other prestigious opportunities. Most students don’t study to achieve something big — they study just enough to get a small job or to satisfy social expectations. Even I was one of them. I didn’t fully understand what IIT really meant — I just kept working hard, perhaps out of habit, hope, or desperation. Somehow, that hard work led me here. I believe students from village don’t lack talent — they lack direction, mentorship, exposure, and awareness.
  • Coming from a rural village, I have seen firsthand how students from financially weaker backgrounds often struggle — not only before entering institutions like IITs, but even after reaching them. At IIT Kanpur, I witnessed many such students facing difficulties, largely because they lacked strong secondary-level education. This is often due to being unable to afford expensive private schools, growing up in villages with limited access to proper schools, or missing quality guidance. Even in villages where high-fee schools exist, the education is often neither smart nor effective. These experiences made me realize that the root of inequality lies not only in college admissions, but much earlier — at the school level, where the foundation of a child’s future is built.
  • I draw my inspiration from Dr. B.R. Ambedkar , Architect of modern India, whose lifelong struggle for justice and equality laid the foundation for a better India. Compared to his sacrifices, our challenges are smaller, yet his ideology guide me to follow his path. I see education—especially through a school—as the most effective way to serve society and uplift the next generation.
