Welcome To The Rising Nalanda

Empowering Ambedkarite Thought, Justice, and Excellence
How we are different from other schools
  • Not for profit Model:
    • we are focusing to educate society instead making profit.
    • Discounts on books, uniforms, bags, and other essentials will be offered to financially weaker families.
    • To reduce financial burden of parents we try not to change books yearly.
  • Advanced and personalized academics:
    • Rising Beyond the Board toward leadership since School is owned by an IITian and Academics is designed by him. Make students to stand in Competitive Environment
    • We focus on brain development along with exams preparation, encouraging students not just to aim for success in exams but to embrace the joy of learning and make the process enjoyable.
    • ensure each student becomes skilled in at least one game (e.g., badminton, chess, basketball) (will be implement later if sufficient fund…)
    • Special Research classes will involve hands-on projects—such as building robots, designing aircraft, and solving complex problems—that require research and critical thinking.
  • National & International Exam Preparation at school level:
    • Early Exposure to JEE, NEET, Olympiad, NTSE, KVPY & other national and International Platforms
    • prepare students for international exams like TOEFL and GRE too, which helps in getting abroad admission which lacks even in high profile schools as far as I have looked into it.
  • Building a Strong Foundation in Technology & Innovation:
    • Comprehensive technology learning & research projects
    • introduction to web development and app development
    • Machine learning
    • Data Structure and algorithm
    • competitive programming
  • We offer open courses:
    • we will offer courses like robotics, machine learning, computer vision, web development, app development, languages(e.g. Japanese (later if fund allowed)) at basic level. Other school’s students can also join these courses.
Some other special quality
  • Future guidance:
    • Guidance by IITian for Engineering students and by AIIMS doctor for medical students will be provided.
  • Inclusive Fee Structure:
    • A 10% income-based fee ensures affordability for all families, encouraging access to quality education irrespective of socio-economic status (will be implemented later T&C apply).
    • We have introduced Forward Fee System(FFS) and Reverse Fee System(RFS) Click here for more Information
  • Holistic Education & Social Values:
    • In addition to academics, students are encouraged to excel in sports, research, and special focus on societal values, shaping them into well-rounded individuals.
  • Parental Involvement & Feedback:
    • Complaint/Suggestion portal for students & parents will be there so that parents can suggest improvements. school will take final decision on that.
Vision & Mission
  • To empower Ambedkarites make them sharp-minded, compassionate, socially responsible, and business-minded individuals who excel in academics, sports, and research. Our vision is to empower students, particularly from reserved categories, to achieve academic excellence on par with the general category in national-level exams and secure positions in top institutions like IITs, AIIMS, and NLU, while also preparing them for global opportunities and entrepreneurial success.
  • Our mission is to establish a school in Greater Noida that provides high-quality education to underprivileged children, focusing on academic excellence and holistic development. We aim to bridge educational disparities by offering resources, guidance, and support to students specially from SC, ST, and OBC(Ambedkarites only) communities. Inspired by the teachings of Dr. B.R. Ambedkar, our goal is to promote educational equity, instill values of compassion and justice, and prepare students to become empowered and responsible citizens.
  • This is our confidential vision_and_mission. Publicly something else will be shown...
Unique Features
  • Excellence Certificate (AEC): Students performing outstanding will get this certificate, this certificate not only honors academic and personal excellence but also offers a tuition fee waiver for new admissions, promoting a cycle of inspiration and equity.
  • Global Language Learning: Students have the opportunity to learn international languages like Japanese, Spanish, and French, expanding their cultural horizons and global communication skills. (will be implemented later).
  • Inclusive Education Rooted in Social Justice: Inspired by Dr. B.R. Ambedkar, we promote an inclusive environment where students from all backgrounds thrive with dignity and equality.
  • Taught by Passionate Educators & IITians: With guidance from mentors who have cleared top exams and walked the same road, students get real insight and direction.
  • Moral Development & Discipline: With a strong focus on values, time respect, and empathy, we shape students into socially responsible human beings, not just high scorers.
Founder's Motivation
  • When I secured admission to IIT Kanpur with a rank around 21,500, I was overjoyed. But I also became aware of the vast disparity in educational opportunities across different social backgrounds. It was a reminder that our community still has a long way to go.
  • I have faced struggles throughout my life, being born into the Scheduled Caste (SC) community. I’ve even worked as a labourer. Experiencing caste discrimination—even at a prestigious institution like IIT Kanpur—deeply affected me and made me reflect on the systemic challenges our community faces. It forced me to look into these problems.
  • I draw my inspiration from B.R. Ambedkar , whose lifelong struggle for justice and equality laid the foundation for a better India. Compared to his sacrifices, our challenges are smaller, yet they guide me to follow his path. I see education—especially through a school—as the most effective way to serve society and uplift the next generation.
  • I am deeply passionate about teaching, and I genuinely love being around children. A school is the one place where I can educate, empower, and play with young minds—making it my ideal space to give back to society.
