Student Social Empowerment Groups

Empowering Nationalism and Awareness

Education & Self-Respect Awareness Group (ESRAG)

🧱 Structure & Roles

Group Leaders Coordinate village visits, awareness campaigns, tutoring, and donation drives
Education Advocates Promote education, equal rights, and conduct dignity workshops for children and parents
Field & Outreach Team Organize street plays, poster drives, distribute pamphlets, and connect with local communities/NGOs
Library & Workshop Organizers Set up book banks, mobile libraries, run workshops for “Why School Matters?”, and connect parents with schools

📢 Activities

  • Village visits: Dialogues on equality and ending exploitation
  • Street plays/poster drives on modern slavery, caste discrimination
  • Booklets/posters with Ambedkar’s teachings, simplified for youth
  • Organize dignity workshops for children and parents
  • Tutoring children in adopted village/slum areas
  • Book collection drives and small libraries
  • Workshops for “Why School Matters?”
  • Literacy sessions for illiterate adults

🎁 Rewards

  • Certificates for active volunteers
  • Wall displays featuring success stories
  • Free books or uniforms to families showing change
  • School bags, stationery, and scholarships for regular attendees
  • “Star Learner” display boards for village/school

💬 Slogans / Insider Lines

  • “Samman ke saath jeena sabka haq hai.”
  • “Mazdoori ka paisa, izzat ki rozi.”

Justice & Rights Group (JRG)

🧱 Structure & Roles

Group Leader Oversees rights seminars & mock parliaments
Legal Educator Simplifies laws, creates learning aids
Story Collector Finds real-life stories of rights, justice
Complaint Helper Supports filling RTI/RTE forms

📢 Activities

  • Mock parliaments and Q&A sessions for youth
  • Legal aid info drives on labor, education, and anti-caste laws
  • Role-play about demanding fair wages, stopping bonded labor

🎁 Rewards

  • Badges or certificates for "Legal Awareness Star"
  • School mentions for kids who assist with real cases

💬 Insider Lines

  • “Kanoon sabke liye barabar hai.”
  • “Haqq ke liye bolna seekho!”

Social Reform & Equality Investigators (SREI)

🧱 Structure & Roles

Team Coordinator Supervises fieldwork, investigations, and reporting
Investigators & Analysts Document injustices, prepare case studies, develop recommendations, support fact-checking and research
Media & Event Creators Create films, posters, stories, organize debates, contests, plays, and exhibitions
Knowledge Anchors Introduce philosophies, encourage critical thinking, host forums on science, logic, and ethics

📢 Activities

  • Identify and document caste-based exclusion, untouchability, or discrimination
  • Create case studies and present at school assemblies
  • Transform findings into plays, films, or printed stories
  • Myth-busting sessions on superstitions and pseudoscience
  • Debates on social topics, essay & poster contests
  • Discussion forums on logic, ethics, and science

🎁 Rewards

  • “Equality Champion” certificates
  • Feature in school media wall or assemblies
  • “Best Debater” medals or books
  • Display thought-provoking posters across campus

💬 Slogan

  • “Bhed-bhav ki deewar girao, sabko ek saman banao!”
  • “Socho, poocho, samjho – anekta mein vikas hai.”
  • “Jigyasa se hi pragati hoti hai.”

Nature, Environment & Health Group (NEHG)

🧱 Structure & Roles

Environment Leadership Guides eco-campaigns, monitors progress, leads tree plantation and plastic-free drives
Field & Water Team Conducts clean-ups, plantations, sustainable farming workshops, and water conservation activities
Health & Hygiene Promoters Coordinate hygiene camps, menstrual health awareness, and cleanliness drives
Wellness & Awareness Anchors Run climate change sessions, first aid training, and anti-addiction campaigns for youth

📢 Activities

  • Tree planting, clean village/river days, plastic-free drives
  • Workshops on water conservation & sustainable farming
  • Climate and disaster awareness sessions
  • Hygiene camps: menstrual and dental hygiene
  • Workshops on alcohol/tobacco/addiction risks
  • Basic disease prevention and first aid training

🎁 Rewards

  • Green badges
  • School environment day awards
  • Health champion awards
  • School wall of hygiene heroes

💬 Line

  • “Pedh lagao, paryavaran bachao!”
  • “Har din, har koi – safai mein yogdan de.”
  • “Sehat pehle, sab kuch baad mein.”
  • “Gandagi bhagao, bimari hatao!”

Peace & Violence-Free Society Group (PVFG)

🧱 Structure & Roles

Peacekeeper Lead Mediates and mentors for harmony
Harmony Historians Collects and documents stories of community unity
Event Facilitator Organizes dialogue, interfaith meets, peace walks
Conflict Resolver Guides students on mediation, non-violence

📢 Activities

  • Peace marches, friendship circles, story exchanges
  • Workshops on conflict-resolution methods for youth
  • Celebrate local festivals together

🎁 Rewards

  • Peacekeeper badges
  • School assembly applause and certificates

💬 Lines

  • “Ekta mein shanti, shanti mein pragati!”
  • “Nafrat nahi, dosti chuno.”

Organizing and Recognizing Group Effort

  • Every group has a mentor (teacher/elder student)
  • Monthly missions and impact tracking
  • Student rotation for new learning opportunities
  • Public recognition: certificates, badges, wall displays, open presentations
