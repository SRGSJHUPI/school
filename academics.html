Academic Structure

Developing sharp minds with a foundation of reasoning, research, and responsibility

Academic Foundations

Core Philosophy and Tuition Structure

  • Brain development by focusing on conceptual learning over rote memorization.
  • Holistic education: developing the whole person-intellectually, emotionally, socially, physically, creatively.
  • Motivation and competition exposure from class 3–5; Ambedkar's story as inspiration and their struggle. How it matters to us.
  • English proficiency prioritized till class 5; other courses adapted as needed.
  • Encouraging debate and inquiry-based learning.
  • Programming and robotics from class 6 onwards.
  • Derivation-based teaching in required courses like Physics, Math.
  • NO option to choose courses in 11th class like PCB, PCM, BSt... Everything will be compulsory.
  • Loop teaching: continuous review of past and current topics.
  • Advance syllabus covering different competitive exams.
  • Annual “Nalanda Challenge You Competition” (NCYC). An inter school competition.

Class-Wise Structure Highlights

Class 1 to Class 3

  • English will be given preference.
  • Play-based learning with focus on language development.
  • Basic mathematics as per syllabus.
  • Ethics, stories, listening, singing and drawing included.

Class 4 to 5

  • Importance of Education will be taught even story of Nelson Mandela, Dr B.R Ambedkar... will be taught to encourage them and to make them aware life without education.
  • English mastery (speaking, writing, reading).
  • Basics of programming languages(c, c++, python...) and Basic programming like Arduino will be taught, logic building puzzles, basic math(according to syllabus) + maths needed to understand basics of programming.
  • Group learning projects and peer quizzes.
  • Ethics(who we are, why we are, why to help other...), listening, singing...

Class 6 to 8

  • All courses mentioned above will be taught in depth.
  • Advance english(targeting GRE/TOEFL)
  • Olympiad/NCERT advanced questions.
  • Programming questions(codeforces, coding ninja...). content from TUF youtube channel will be followed.
  • Basic research projects. Robotics (Lectures on NPTEL will be followed).

Class 9 to 12

  • Competitive exam focus: JEE, NEET, Olympiads, GRE, UPSC basics.
  • Laptops for research, programming, and projects.
  • Dedicated research project for every student from class 9 onward.

Assessment & Exam Preparation

Exam Strategies

Although Exams are important to measure one's potential but we believe Exams are not the ultimate measure of a student’s ability. Along with exams our focus is on real learning and development, not just performance on paper. We help students during exams to test understanding, application, and logic-not memory. Exams are checkpoints, not pressure points. We value project work, research, group collaboration, and communication skills along with exams.

Internal Examination System

  • Minor Exams: Monthly, covering current loop topics only.
  • Major Exams: Twice a year, testing past and present concepts.
  • Ultimate Exams: Yearly exam reviewing all topics from Nursery to current class.
  • Group Exams: Rotating teams of 5–6; group average counts as final grade.
  • General Exam: Covers all classroom discussions, to motivate attentive learning.

Competitive Exam Preparation

  • Olympiads (Math, Science, English, GK), NTSE and NMMS prep from Class 6 onwards.
  • JEE Advanced and NEET track from Class 9 with problem-solving.
  • GRE, SAT, TOEFL intro from Class 11 for international opportunities.
  • UPSC awareness through Ethics and Political Science modules.

Nalanda Challenge You Competition (NCYC)

Annual competition open for all other school's students too, that challenges students on:

  • Problem-solving in Math, Logic, and Real-life Scenarios
  • Speaking, Critical Thinking, and Social Awareness
  • Designing Science/Tech models or solutions for village-level issues
  • Top scorers will receive Certificates (along with Dr. Ambedkar gold statue) and public recognition.

Response Certificates(AEC)

  • In 6th,7th, 8th, 10th, and 12th classes AEC will be awarded.
  • Rated from 1* to 5* based on: sharp-mindedness, discipline, leadership, empathy
  • Used for scholarships, admission discounts, and portfolio enhancement
